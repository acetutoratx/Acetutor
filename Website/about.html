Meet the Founders

Aarav Singh

SAT: 1570 | GPA: 5.8

Aarav Singh is the co-founder and operations director of ACE Tutoring. With a 1570 SAT score, a 5.8 GPA, and 5s on all AP exams, he combines academic excellence with strong leadership and organizational skills. Aarav manages client relations, scheduling, and business operations while tutoring students in many subjects and test preparation. Known for his structure-driven teaching and strategic mindset, he focuses on helping students learn how to learn—building confidence, efficiency, and lasting understanding.

Charity Kao

SAT: 1560 | GPA: 5.8

Charity Kao is the co-founder and academic director of ACE Tutoring. With a 1560 SAT score, a 5.8+ GPA, and 5s on all AP exams, she leads tutor training, academic quality, and student learning development. Charity is known for her clarity, patience, and ability to simplify complex topics while maintaining academic rigor. She ensures every ACE tutor upholds the company’s high standards of excellence and empathy, guiding students to achieve consistent, meaningful academic growth.

ACE Tutoring was founded by Aarav Singh and Charity Kao, two top-performing students from Westwood High School in Austin, Texas. Driven by a shared belief that students learn best from those who've recently mastered the same material, they built ACE to make high-quality, personalized tutoring more relatable, effective, and accessible.

With Aarav leading operations and client management, and Charity overseeing academic development and tutor training, the two balance business precision with educational excellence. Their combined leadership ensures that every ACE session reflects the company's core values — Achievement, Clarity, and Empowerment — and that every student receives the guidance, structure, and confidence they need to thrive.

Our Mission

Our Vision

As Aarav and Charity graduate, they’ll pass the torch to the next generation of high-achieving Austin students—ensuring ACE remains a sustainable, student-led network of excellence.
